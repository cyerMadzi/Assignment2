DOM Manipulation Game

Welcome to our "DOM Manipulation" game, A game in which your skills will be tested as a grid of 16 total playing cards will be preseneted before you and your job is to identify which 2 pair cards match .

Playing Cards

The grid consists of 16 cards, each with a unique number. Your task is to find the matching pairs by clicking on the cards. When you click on a card, it will flip over to reveal its number. If you find a matching pair, they will stay flipped over. If not, they will flip back after a short delay.
