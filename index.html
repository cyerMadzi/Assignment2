DOM Manipulation Game

Welcome to our "DOM Manipulation" game. A game in which your skills will be tested as a grid of 16 total playing cards will be presented before you and your job is to identify which 2 cards match.

Playing Cards

The grid consists of 16 cards, each with a hidden letter. Your task is to find the matching pairs by clicking on the cards. When you click on a card, it will flip to reveal its letter. If two cards match, they stay flipped. If not, they flip back after a short delay.
